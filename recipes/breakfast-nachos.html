Breakfast Nachos Recipe

Ingredients

  • Breakfast Chips
  • Breakfast Meat
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Jalapenos
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Cook the breakfast meat
  2. Put the chips on a plate
  3. Put the breakfast meat on the chips
  4. Cover the mixture in cheese
  5. Add jalapenos
  6. Warm it up to melt the cheese
  7. Put sour cream to taste
